BudgetEngine is built for simplicity on 4 fundamental concepts:

  • Accounts
  • Revenues
  • Expenses
  • Projections

Accounts contain your posted transactions, revenues, expenses, and projections. Revenues are the money you earn. Expenses are the money you spend. Projections are the future money you expect to earn or spend.

If you keep BudgetEngine updated with the transactions that you post to your account, it can project what your account balance will be in the future, helping keep you on track.
